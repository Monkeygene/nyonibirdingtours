Birding in Africa

Imagine the African sun setting over the horizon, painting fiery hues across the sky as you take a sip of your favorite beverage, the ice clinking softly against the glass. The air is fresh with the earthy scent of the bush, and birdsong fills the late afternoon air. Around you, fellow birders share stories of the day's adventures. And the best part? Nyoni Birding Safaris

Welcome to Nyoni Birding Safaris. We specialize in South & East African birding, offering exclusive, small-group tours of around six guests, designed for birding enthusiasts. Your birding adventure will be led by expert guide and birding enthusiast Eugene Booysen. Your tour will be immersive, with unforgettable encounters with Africa’s most sought-after bird species and even wildlife.

With a focus on quality, personal attention, and deep regional expertise, we ensure that every moment in the field counts. Whether you are looking to spot endemic species, complement the eyes with stunning locations, or enjoy an intimate birding adventure with fellow birders, we craft experiences that become part of your checklist.
